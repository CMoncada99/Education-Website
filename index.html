The aforementioned project was successfully completed during the Fall 2021 semester. The project involved a comprehensive analysis of multiple Spotify data sets, leveraging data integration techniques to identify similarities and differences among them. The schema provided a visual representation of the table connections, while the data dictionary offered a detailed description of the values within each table. Additionally, the project encompassed the development of both simple and complex queries to facilitate diverse data analysis tasks. A particular aspect of the project delved into an experimental topic that I personally found intriguing, providing in-depth insights. Furthermore, the concluding section of the project showcased the application of data visualization techniques to perform detailed analysis and gain valuable insights from the database. This demonstrated my ability to analyze databases and present information in a user-friendly manner. In the context of making informed business decisions, it is imperative to present data in a manner that is easily comprehensible to stakeholders who may not possess an extensive understanding of the underlying data. Thus, the project emphasized the significance of creating clear and intelligible visualizations, ensuring that decision-makers can utilize the visualized data to make informed choices.

Clicking on the this picture will take you to the Project Management assignment.

This project represents a significant achievement in the Project Management course, undertaken during the Spring 2022 semester. The project focuses on a comprehensive analysis of outsourcing within the information technology industry. It delves into the historical context of outsourcing, explores its benefits, examines its drawbacks, and provides guidance on when and why a company should consider utilizing outsourcing or refrain from doing so. To enrich the analysis, the project incorporates insights from both current and former employees of outsourcing companies, capturing their firsthand experiences, including both positive and negative aspects. These insights contribute to a well-rounded understanding of the outsourcing landscape and its impact on business operations. The findings of this project hold relevance beyond the specific context of the information technology industry. The tools and methods employed in outsourcing have become widely adopted in various business sectors, particularly in the information technology field. Therefore, understanding the dynamics and considerations surrounding outsourcing is valuable knowledge for professionals operating within the broader business environment.
